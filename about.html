Mikhail Tal

The Magician from Riga (1936-1992)

Mikhail Tal was the 8th World Chess Champion, known for his incredibly aggressive and imaginative playing style. He became World Champion in 1960 at age 23 by defeating Mikhail Botvinnik. Tal was famous for his brilliant sacrifices and his ability to create complex tactical positions that confused his opponents.

About This Game
